Tower Defense Game

Level: 1 | Money: $1000 | Lives: 20 | Wave: 0/3 | Total Score: 0

How to Play:

  • Click on a tower button to select a tower type.
  • Click on the game field to place the tower (avoiding the path).
  • Towers will automatically attack enemies within their range.
  • Survive all waves and levels to win!
  • New tower types unlock as you progress through levels.

Tips:

  • Place towers strategically to maximize their effectiveness.
  • Different towers have different strengths - experiment with combinations!
  • Position Slow Towers near the entrance to slow enemies early.
  • Place high-damage towers like Snipers where they can hit enemies for longer periods.
